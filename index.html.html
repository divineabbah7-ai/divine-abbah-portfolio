I'm a Mechanical Design Engineer with over 4 years of hands-on experience delivering detailed engineering designs across structural steel, mechanical assemblies, and industrial fit-out projects.

As a Certified SolidWorks Professional (CSWP), I work across the full design lifecycle — from initial concept and 3D modelling through to fabrication drawings, BOM generation, and site coordination.

My project portfolio spans steel structures, warehouse systems, staircases, handrails, sheet metal, mechanical components, and bespoke fabrications — with a consistent focus on manufacturability and accuracy.
